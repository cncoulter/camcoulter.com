Hello!

I'm a writer and accessibility nerd, among other things, and I think a lot about ethical technology, speculative fiction, and intentional community.

I blog about social justice, simple living, community, and spirituality for The Ruined Report, and I sometimes write about science fiction and fantasy for Skiffy and Fanty. I also blog on this site, usually about books or accessibility.

Online, I'm more of a “blogs & email” person than a “social media” person. If you like what I write, subscribe to my blog or send me an email and say hi. Thanks for stopping by!

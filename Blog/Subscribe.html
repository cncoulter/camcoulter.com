What are web feeds?

Web feeds allow you to follow a website or blog. RSS feeds are the most well-known standard for this, but there are other formats as well, such as Atom feeds and JSON feeds. With a feed reader app, you can subscribe to different web feeds, get notified when there are new posts, read posts of course, and personalize how content is displayed in a way that's comfortable for you. There are many different feed reader apps out there, and some are desktop apps while others are mobile apps or web apps. For more about subscribing to web feeds, see my post “Use RSS Feeds” on The Ruined Report.
